## 6. ISF & Community Shader Ecosystem

### ISF (Interactive Shader Format)
- **Standard**: Open format by Vidvox, used by VDMX, CoGe, Magic, Resolume
- **Registry**: [interactiveshaderformat.com](https://interactiveshaderformat.com) — 1000+ community shaders
- **Input types**: float, bool, color, point2D, image, audioFFT, audio
- **ExperienceMusic status**: ISFAdapter.ts implemented

### MilkDrop / Butterchurn
- **Library**: 100,000+ presets from Winamp/MilkDrop community
- **Butterchurn**: WebGL 2.0 renderer by jberg, npm package
- **Curated set**: butterchurn-presets includes ~200 high-quality presets
- **ExperienceMusic status**: MilkdropRenderer + Bridge implemented

### Shadertoy / GLSL Sandbox
- **Shadertoy**: 90,000+ shaders, many audio-reactive (iChannel0 = audio texture)
- **Conversion**: Requires remapping iResolution, iTime, iChannel uniforms
- **ExperienceMusic potential**: Build ShadertoyAdapter similar to ISFAdapter

### Key Community Resources
- [The Book of Shaders](https://thebookofshaders.com) — GLSL fundamentals
